## The Daily "Zero-Touch" SOP (Standard Operating Procedure)

This is how you manage the empire in under 2 hours a day.

### Phase 1: The Weekly Strategy Session (1 Hour - Sunday)
1.  **Review Analytics:** Look at YouTube CTR (Click-Through Rate) and AVD (Average View Duration). Look at X impressions.
2.  **Topic Selection:** Review the 3 topics Manus generated for the week. Approve 2.
3.  **Script Generation:** Instruct Manus to write the scripts, SEO metadata, and X threads for the approved topics.

### Phase 2: Asset Production (2-3 Hours - Monday/Wednesday)
1.  **Voiceover:** Drop the Manus-generated script into ElevenLabs (or record it yourself).
2.  **Visuals:** Generate necessary B-roll using Midjourney or record screen captures of the tools in action.
3.  **Editing:** Drop audio and visuals into CapCut. Add auto-captions and dynamic zooms. Export.
4.  **Thumbnails:** Use Figma/Canva to create 2 thumbnail variations based on Manus's concepts.

### Phase 3: The Daily Execution (30-45 Minutes - Daily)
1.  **Publishing (If upload day):** Upload the video to YouTube. Paste the Manus-generated title, description, and tags. Upload both thumbnails for A/B testing.
2.  **Scheduling:** Schedule the Manus-generated X thread to go live 2 hours after the YouTube video. Schedule the daily short X posts.
3.  **Engagement (Crucial):** Spend 15 minutes replying to comments on YouTube. Spend 15 minutes replying to large accounts in the SaaS/AI niche on X (The 70/30 reply strategy).
4.  **Review:** Check the A/B test results on the YouTube thumbnail after 3 hours. Switch if necessary.
